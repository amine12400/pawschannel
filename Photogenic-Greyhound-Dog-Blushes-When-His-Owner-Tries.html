Photogenic Greyhound Dog Blushes When His Owner Tries to Take Pictures With Him

Photos are e good way to stop the time and lock the memory for good. We all get excited when the picture time arrives sometimes we are very bold and up for it sometimes we get all shy and quirky.

The coolest photos are those when you make certain moves or funny facial expressions but how about when you totally melt while into the shoot. And that’s the case with Sparky, a three-legged Italian greyhound who obviously melts when the spotlight shines on him. His reaction is likened to Jell-O when Kennedy Simmons, his current owner, tries to pose with him for a cute photo. But do not make the mistake of thinking that he’s not photogenic.

This aforementioned model has a lot to offer, his charismatic sight has taken everyone’s heart. Thousand of followers on IG are appreciating his adorable greyhound figure.

Kennedy Simons is a vet that has adopted sparky and since then they have become inseparable. I one of his interviews he Simons stated that: “Sparky is so fun to be around. He is the biggest goofball, but he’s also very mischievous.

If you don’t have eyes on him, he gets up on the table and snags food, or goes and grabs my socks from the hamper. Sometimes I describe him as a sour patch kid because he will snag my food from the table, but then just come curl into my lap right after, as if he didn’t eat my meal. He is very fearless!”

However sparky is that type of dog that doesn’t like to be in the spotlight and does everything in his power to avoid it.

“I adopted Sparky from a client at work (I’m a veterinary assistant). Sparky had broken his leg with his previous family and got surgery, but because they had seen him break his leg and it traumatized them a little bit, they were too scared to let him do anything once he had healed from the surgery, so they decided that he needed to have a puppyhood with someone that wouldn’t be scared to let him do stuff.”
